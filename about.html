Web Developement

Web development is the process involved in building an Internet site (World Wide Web) or an intranet (a private network). Among Web professionals, "web development" usually refers to the main non-design section of building Web sites: marking and code. Every Web Developer should have a basic understood of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Responsive Web Design is used in all forms of modern web development. Graphic Designing

Graphic design is a craft in which professionals create visual content to convey messages. Although working in the digital age means you have to design with interactive software, the imagery still goes round around the old principles. As a photographer, you need to have a solid understanding of color theory and how important it is to make the right choice of color scheme. Color options should reflect not only the organization (e.g., blue suits) but also what users expect (e.g., red with alerts; green for notifications to continue).

Freelancing

Freelancing is a place where you take your skills to work. You may be wondering, "Now what is freelancing?" Freelancing means working as a private company rather than being hired by someone else. Freelancers are self-employed and are often mentioned as independent contractors. With the growth of the gig economy, people are talking about freelancing than ever before. And that is because today there are more freelancers than ever before in history - a 2019 study by the Up-work and Freelancing Union shows that 57 more than Americans are self-employed by 2019. And according to the same study, 53% of employees are 18-22 years old. They are freelancers.
